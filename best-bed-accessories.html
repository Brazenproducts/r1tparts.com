Best Bed Accessories for the Rivian R1T

Tonneau covers, bed racks, bed liners, and cargo solutions reviewed for the R1T's 54-inch bed.

1. Tesmanian Hard Tri-Fold Tonneau Cover

The Tesmanian is a popular aftermarket hard tonneau for the R1T. Its tri-fold aluminum panel design sits flush with the bed rails and provides weather-sealed cargo protection. Installation uses a clamp-on system with no drilling required.

Pros

  • Hard aluminum panels for security
  • Weather-sealed gasket system
  • No-drill clamp installation
  • Tri-fold design for partial bed access

Cons

  • Higher price point vs soft covers
  • Adds some weight to the bed
  • Limited headroom when folded up

2. YAKIMA OverHaul HD Truck Bed Rack

YAKIMA's OverHaul HD is a heavy-duty adjustable bed rack system. It's compatible with the R1T bed dimensions and supports up to 500 lbs of dynamic load. Works with rooftop tents, cargo boxes, kayak mounts, and other accessories.

Pros

  • 500 lb dynamic load capacity
  • Height-adjustable crossbars
  • Compatible with T-slot accessories
  • Powder-coated aluminum

Cons

  • Premium pricing
  • May require R1T-specific adapter kit
  • Heavy when fully assembled

3. BedRug Classic Bed Liner

BedRug's carpet-like bed liner provides cushioned protection for the R1T bed floor. The closed-cell foam construction won't absorb water and dries quickly. It protects cargo from sliding and protects the bed from scratches and dents.

Pros

  • Soft surface protects cargo
  • Won't absorb water
  • Insulates against heat and cold
  • Custom-cut for specific truck beds

Cons

  • Harder to clean than spray-in liners
  • May require trimming for R1T fit
  • Not as rugged as hard liners
